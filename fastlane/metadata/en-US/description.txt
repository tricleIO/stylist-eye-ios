StylistEye gives you the opportunity to have unprecedented access to your favorite fashion bloggers, stylists and fashionistas. This service aims to solve a simple problem: shopping for clothes in stores is overwhelming and inconvenient and often frustrating. Get styled to your taste, budget and lifestyle - from anywhere in the world by your favorite bloggers and stylists. Let them help you to organise and update your wardrobe.

- You will get your wardrobe organized and updated with new clothes fitting your body, style and wallet
- You will get recommendations about your body type and what best fits your shape as well as what you should throw away
- Take the frustration out of shopping by being simply directed to your purchase recommendations online
- A well planned and selected wardrobe saves your energy, time, money and space in your wardrobe
- We only work with the best proven fashionistas from all over the world, offering a range of contacts to suit your budget.
